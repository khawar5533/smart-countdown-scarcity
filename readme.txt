Display time-limited, product-specific sale banners on WooCommerce product pages, showing discount details and countdown timers only during active sale periods to boost urgency and drive conversions.

== Description ==

**Smart Countdown Scarcity** helps you boost conversions and reduce cart abandonment by displaying dynamic, time-limited sale banners with countdown timers on WooCommerce product pages. The plugin automatically detects when a product is on sale and shows a countdown timer with discount information, creating a sense of urgency to encourage faster purchases.

**Key Features**

* Display product-specific sale banners only during active sale periods.
* Dynamic countdown timers for each sale.
* Customize banner appearance and messaging.
* Boost urgency and scarcity to drive conversions.
* Works seamlessly with WooCommerce sales scheduling.

== Requirements ==

* WooCommerce 9.9.4 or newer.
* WordPress 6.7 or newer.

== Installation ==

**1. Install and activate the plugin**

1. In your WordPress dashboard, navigate to **Plugins > Add New**.
2. Search for “Smart Countdown Scarcity”.
3. Click **Install Now**, and then **Activate** the plugin.

**2. Configure your settings**
1. Go to **Countdown Setting**.
2. Customize your banner appearance form
3.add the product information using form.
4. Save your changes.
5.Select the radio button from backend and the selected product information shows on banner
